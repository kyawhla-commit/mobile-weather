### üêÑ Cow Urine Based Fertilizers

#### Cow Urine Fertilizer (Jeevamrut)
- **NPK Ratio**: 1-0.5-1.7
- **Type**: Liquid fermented fertilizer
- **Fermentation**: 7-10 days
- **Traditional Use**: Ancient Indian farming practice

**Ingredients**:
- 10 liters fresh cow urine
- 10 kg cow dung
- 2 kg jaggery (gur)
- 2 kg gram flour (besan)
- Handful of living soil
- 200 liters water for dilution

**Benefits**:
- Rich in growth hormones
- Improves soil microbiology
- Enhances plant immunity
- Natural pest deterrent
- Increases soil fertility
- Promotes root development
